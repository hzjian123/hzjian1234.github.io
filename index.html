Deep HDR with multiple input images

HDR is widely used in our smartphones but image quality is limited by the sensor size so computation photography became popular after Google released HDR+. Current deep learning based methods achieve great results but with the supervision of Wavelet Transform, performance can be further enhanced.

RTL design for ML acceleration

RTL design is about verifying whether a design is a good ideal for a FPGA. Notice hardware acceleration for ML is popular nowadays such as Google's TPU. We aim to map the computation of Neural Network to a FPGA and perform required tasks (convolution & ReLu). Tons of innovations can be made here such as Clock Gating or RAM management.

Video understating by Self-supervised Learning

TikTok is a global trending right now and billions of video are uploaded everyday so censorship and sorting becomes an impossible job for human. Due to the nature of video, it is impossible to use the same method from image domain to train a small Neural Network. instead, we select crops from the original video and network is required to judge whether 2 crops are from the same source or not. in addition, we introduce background swapping here to focus on the main object.

Learn more

Real-time arm tracking by Hidden Markov Model and Complementary Filter

Real-time tracking spatial location of your arms is meaningful because you might want your smartwatch to evaluate your exercise more precisely. Different from depth camera such as the MS Kinect, smartwatch uses limited information to calculate the spatial coordinate. An elegant approach is to build an arm model and use Hidden Markov Model to estimate with "hidden variables" include acceleration and angular speed. HMM also boosts performance of ML based approach.
